Hey There.
I'm Mary.

I am an African American entrepreneur, nonprofit founder, content creator, and the woman who got tired of having quiet conversations alone in her head and decided to say them out loud instead.

"My journey through the isolating experience of being a single, brown-skinned woman navigating predominantly white spaces, while entering my 50s, inspired me to create Fifty and Unfiltered."

With a background in civil rights compliance, contract compliance, and public administration spanning more than 18 years, I spent my career advocating for others. Now I am advocating for us.

Our Purpose

The Mission Behind Fifty and Unfiltered

Fifty and Unfiltered exists because Black women over 50 deserve a space that reflects our real lives. Not the sanitized version. The actual truth of what it means to navigate love, loss, health, money, and identity when the world expects us to be invisible.

Here, we are not invisible. We are the whole conversation. We hold each other up and refuse to let anyone rush us through this chapter.

"Your story and your strength matter. And I want to hear it."

Mary Geter
Background

More Than a Blog

Mary holds a Master of Science in Counseling Education from Mississippi State University and a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from Mississippi University for Women.

She spent more than 18 years with the Jackson Municipal Airport Authority in contract compliance, civil rights, and public administration. She is the founder of Chat Chew and Do Empowerment Women Group, a Mississippi-based nonprofit and the proud sponsor of Fifty and Unfiltered.

Who We Are

Chat Chew and Do Empowerment Women Group, known as CCDEW, is a Mississippi-based nonprofit organization founded to empower women through community building, honest conversation, and collective action. We believe that when women come together, share their truths, and support one another, they change not just their own lives but the world around them.

CCDEW is the heart behind Fifty and Unfiltered. The same values that drive our community work are the foundation this platform is built on.

💬

Chat

We create safe spaces for honest, unfiltered conversation about the issues affecting women's lives today.

🍴

Chew

We dig into the real issues with depth and substance. Health, relationships, finances, identity.

✌️

Do

We move from conversation to action, building programs and resources that create real change for women.

Why We Support Fifty and Unfiltered

Fifty and Unfiltered represents everything CCDEW stands for. It centers Black women. It refuses to minimize their experiences. It tells the truth about what life after 50 actually looks like, and it builds community around that truth. CCDEW is proud to stand behind every word of it.
